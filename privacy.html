Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

This site, capa-language.com, is a static website built to respect the privacy of the people who visit it. It runs no analytics, performs no tracking, and sets no cookies of its own. The page you are reading is served under a strict Content-Security-Policy (default-src 'self'), which means the browser is told to load resources and scripts only from this site and to block third-party scripts and assets outright.

What this site collects

Directly, nothing. There is no analytics platform, there are no tracking pixels, there are no forms that submit your data anywhere, and there are no first-party cookies. The site does not build a profile of you and has nothing to sell or share, because it never gathers anything in the first place.

Hosting and delivery

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages and served through Cloudflare, which acts as the content delivery network in front of it. To deliver and protect the site, these services necessarily process some technical data for each request, such as the visitor's IP address, the user-agent string the browser sends, and the date and time of the request. That processing is governed by their own privacy policies rather than by this site:

  • GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy

As part of protecting the site, Cloudflare may set strictly necessary cookies for security and abuse mitigation, for example the __cf_bm cookie. These are not used for advertising and are not used to track visitors across sites.

Cookies

This site sets no cookies of its own. Any cookie you may encounter while visiting comes from Cloudflare and is strictly necessary for security, not for tracking. There are no advertising cookies and no analytics cookies.

Your rights (GDPR)

The author of this site is based in the European Union. The technical processing carried out by GitHub and Cloudflare to serve and protect the site rests on the legitimate interest of delivering and securing the website.

Visitors have the rights granted by the General Data Protection Regulation, including the rights of access, rectification, erasure, and objection. Because the site itself stores no personal data, requests about the technical data processed during delivery are best directed to the policies of GitHub and Cloudflare linked above. For any privacy question, you can also reach the author at the contact address below.
