Privacy

Privacy policy

capa-language.com is a static website built to respect the privacy of the people who visit it. It runs no analytics, performs no tracking, and sets no cookies of its own. The page is served under a strict Content-Security-Policy (default-src 'self'), so the browser blocks third-party scripts and assets outright.

Last updated: 2026-06-24

What this site collects

Directly, nothing. There is no analytics platform, no tracking pixels, no forms that submit your data anywhere, and no first-party cookies. The site does not build a profile of you and has nothing to sell or share, because it never gathers anything in the first place.

Hosting and delivery

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages and served through Cloudflare as the CDN in front of it. To deliver and protect the site, these services necessarily process some technical data for each request: the visitor's IP address, the user-agent string, and the date and time of the request. That processing is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy

As part of protecting the site, Cloudflare may set strictly necessary cookies for security and abuse mitigation (for example __cf_bm). These are not used for advertising and not used to track visitors across sites.

Cookies

This site sets no cookies of its own. Any cookie you may encounter comes from Cloudflare and is strictly necessary for security, not tracking. There are no advertising cookies and no analytics cookies.

Your rights (GDPR)

The author of this site is based in the European Union. The technical processing carried out by GitHub and Cloudflare to serve and protect the site rests on the legitimate interest of delivering and securing the website. Visitors have the rights granted by the GDPR: access, rectification, erasure, and objection. Because the site itself stores no personal data, requests about the technical data processed during delivery are best directed to the policies of GitHub and Cloudflare above.

Contact & changes

For any privacy question, you can reach the author at the contact address listed in the repository. This policy may be updated over time; the "Last updated" date near the top reflects the version currently in force.
